For Farmers

The Problem: Existing System

For Farmers

The conventional supply chain involves multiple intermediaries, which significantly reduces the profits for farmers. Farmers often lack direct access to a wider market and have little control over pricing. This leaves them vulnerable to low payments and uncertain demand.

For Consumers

Consumers face higher prices due to the long chain of middlemen. The system lacks transparency, and delays often result in spoilage of perishable goods, compromising freshness and quality by the time produce reaches the customer.

The Solution: Proposed System

Direct Access for Farmers

Our platform eliminates intermediaries, offering farmers direct access to consumers. Farmers can set their own prices, manage listings, and sell produce directly, ensuring better profitability and faster payments.

Fresh & Fair for Consumers

Consumers get fresher products at reasonable prices. A transparent rating and review system helps buyers make informed choices, while local delivery reduces transit time and spoilage — building trust and a direct link to the source of their food.

How Farmers Join

Farmers can sign up, create a profile, and list products using the Farmer Dashboard. We provide simple tools to manage inventory, pricing, and orders — plus an easy-to-use sales view so farmers can track their revenue and order history.
