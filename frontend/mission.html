Our Mission

What is Kisaan Dost?

Kisaan Dost is a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers by creating a direct-to-consumer agricultural marketplace.

Our mission is to empower farmers with digital tools to sell their produce fairly and transparently, eliminating middlemen and maximizing profits for producers.

We aim to build a sustainable, tech-driven ecosystem that benefits both farmers and customers — ensuring fresh, organic produce reaches every household while supporting India’s agricultural backbone.

Farming made simple. From soil to sale.
