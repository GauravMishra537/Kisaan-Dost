Q: What is Kisaan Dost?

A: Kisaan Dost is an online platform that connects farmers directly with customers, helping farmers sell their fresh produce without middlemen and allowing customers to buy farm-fresh products at fair prices.

Q: Who can use Kisaan Dost?

A: Both farmers and customers can register. Farmers can list their products for sale, and customers can browse and purchase directly from them.
