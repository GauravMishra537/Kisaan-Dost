How It Works

Farm-to-Table in 3 Simple Steps

1. Farmers List Produce

Local farmers list their fresh produce, set their own prices, and manage their inventory directly on our platform.

2. You Place an Order

You browse fresh, local produce by category or farmer, add items to your cart, and place your order with just a few clicks.

3. Fresh Delivery

The farmer receives your order and handles the delivery, ensuring you get the freshest produce, often picked the same day.
