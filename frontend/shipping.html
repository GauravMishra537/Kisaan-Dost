Shipping & Delivery

← Back to Home

We work with local logistics partners and small carriers to deliver fresh produce from farmers to your door. Below are the details about shipping areas, timelines and fees.

Delivery Areas

Currently we deliver within selected cities and surrounding regions. When placing an order you will be able to enter your full address — the system checks whether delivery is available to your pincode at checkout.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • Same-day delivery: Available for selected nearby zip codes if the order is placed before 10:00 AM.
  • Next-day delivery: Standard for most locations when ordered before 6:00 PM.
  • Rural / remote areas: 2–3 business days depending on logistics partner availability.

Shipping Fees

We apply a small shipping fee for short-distance deliveries. In many locations we offer free shipping for orders above a threshold (example: ₹500).

Example policy:
  • Orders < ₹200: Delivery charge ₹10
  • Orders ≥ ₹200: Free delivery

Packaging & Quality

We pack perishables carefully to preserve freshness — insulated boxes for sensitive items and produce-friendly packing. If you receive an item that doesn't meet expectations, please contact support within 24 hours and we’ll resolve it.
